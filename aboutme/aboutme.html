Adam Danial Ingah (born July 3, 2003), is an American. I don't feel like writing the rest of this article in third person, so I won't because it's my page and I can do whatever I want!!!

Anyway, I am a graduate student at the University of Washington pursuing my M.S. in Electrical Engineering. I have many interests and hobbies that I can't even begin to list them all here. I try to expose myself to everything life has to offer.

I grew up in Aurora, Colorado (pretty much Denver). Of all the places on Earth, that city definitely feels the most like "home" to me. I recently moved to Seattle after spending four years in Alabama. It seems like the south never fully recovered from losing the Civil War, so I am glad to end up in the Pacific Northwest.

One question I get a lot is: "Where are you from?". Most people think I'm Filipino, which is sort of true. My mom is from Malaysia, and my dad is from Indonesia. I'm not sure what that makes me, but I tell people one or the other if they really pry.

I took a DNA test once and it came back from all sorts of places, one of which was filipino (14%). I kept checking every other year and that percentage kept increasing by 1%. Whatever the hell that means. I guess the most accurate thing to say is "Austronesian". At the end of the day, all the islander/archipelago guys and gals are more alike than they are different. Don't kill me for saying that.

What else is there to know? I have three sisters. Two older one younger. We're close-ish (2-4 year age gap) in age. Zero brothers. I'm sure there's more to know about me. Can't quite think of anything. My favorite color is green.
