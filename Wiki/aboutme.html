Adam Ingah

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about Adam Ingah, the American. There is another Adam Ingah, who is from Nigeria.

This is a blue pencil

Adam Danial Ingah (born July 3, 2003), is an American. Ingah was born in Virginia, but moved to Denver, Colorado (more specifically Aurora) at an early age. It's actually pretty difficult to write this in third person, so I'm just going to write this in first person for the rest of the article. Sorry. Anyways - I spent most of my life Aurora. Because I've just bounced across the U.S. a whole bunch, I think Aurora is the closest place on Earth I can call home.
